# Foot Solutions Bot — Strategy

## Goal

Build a world-class AI sales assistant for Foot Solutions — the #1 foot wellness franchise in the US — and use it as a demo pitch to Bryan Scott (co-owner, EVP of Strategic Growth) to land a Kepram AI consulting engagement.

Bryan Scott is a former NFL player (Atlanta Falcons, 2003), Shark Tank winner (deal with Mark Cuban + Daymond John), and co-owner of Foot Solutions based in Atlanta. We know Bryan from BNI. The demo link will be sent to him as a "check this out" with the ask being a meeting to discuss building this for real.

## Why this bot is different from Trinity CRE

Trinity CRE's bot is primarily a **lead qualifier** — the goal is a short conversation that ends with "schedule a call with Burke." High-stakes ($500K+ deals), customers won't trust a bot for the full decision.

Foot Solutions customers often need **detailed, scientific answers** before they buy. The product is tangible, the stakes are lower, and a bot can actually provide MORE accurate information than a salesperson trained in a few days. This bot is designed to:

1. Answer detailed foot health questions with clinical accuracy
2. Recommend specific products by name and model number
3. Upsell toward custom orthotics ($400–$500, the highest-margin product) naturally
4. Handle off-hours traffic when no staff are in the store
5. Serve as a 24/7 sales associate that never misses a lead

## Personas we expect

- **The sufferer**: "I've had plantar fasciitis for 6 months. Nothing works. What do I do?"
- **The researcher**: "What's the biomechanical difference between custom orthotics and a prefab insole?"
- **The shopper**: "I need shoes for standing 8 hours at work. What do you have?"
- **The skeptic**: "Why should I spend $400 on orthotics when I can get insoles at CVS for $30?"
- **The medical referral**: "My podiatrist told me to get custom orthotics. Can you help me with that?"
- **The chronic pain sufferer**: "My knees hurt. Could my feet be causing it?"
- **The athlete**: "I'm a runner. I keep getting shin splints."
