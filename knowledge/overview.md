# Foot Solutions — Company Overview

## Who We Are

Foot Solutions is the #1 foot wellness franchise in the United States, founded in 2000. With 70+ locations worldwide — including 5 stores across Georgia — we are dedicated to one thing: keeping feet healthy, pain-free, and properly supported.

Our tagline: **"The Foot Wellness Experts."**
Our mission: Improve Balance. Correct Alignment. Relieve Pain.
Our vision: To educate and empower customers with the health benefits of custom orthotics and supportive footwear.

We "own foot wellness" — unlike big-box retailers or medical offices, Foot Solutions combines expert clinical knowledge with a curated product selection in a neighborhood retail setting. Board-certified pedorthists staff our locations and provide personalized assessments that walk-in shoe stores simply cannot match.

## About Bryan Scott

Bryan Scott is co-owner and EVP of Strategic Growth at Foot Solutions, based in Atlanta, Georgia.

Before Foot Solutions, Bryan had a decorated 10-year NFL career — drafted 55th overall in the 2003 NFL Draft by the Atlanta Falcons, then played for the New Orleans Saints and Buffalo Bills. He received the 2012 Buffalo Bills Walter Payton Man of the Year award for his community service work.

Post-NFL, Bryan appeared on Shark Tank pitching a shock-absorbing insole product, securing a deal with Mark Cuban and Daymond John. That business was subsequently acquired by Foot Solutions, Inc., and Bryan remained to lead growth and franchise development.

Bryan is also an accomplished musician (piano, drums), released a single titled "DEDICATED," and performed with the NFL Players Choir on America's Got Talent (Top 5 finish). He lives in Atlanta with his wife Maisha and four daughters.

Bryan's philosophy on business: "We are always continuously finding solutions so people can return to doing the things they love most."

## What Makes Foot Solutions Different

| What Others Offer | What Foot Solutions Offers |
|---|---|
| Generic off-the-shelf insoles | Custom 3D-printed orthotics made for your feet specifically |
| Salespeople with limited training | Board-certified pedorthists (foot care specialists) |
| Shoes picked for style | Shoes selected and fitted for your foot type and condition |
| No follow-up | Ongoing support, adjustments, and guidance |

**Medical community trust:** Hundreds of podiatrists, orthopedists, chiropractors, rheumatologists, and physical therapists refer patients to Foot Solutions annually. We work alongside the medical community — not against it.

**Proprietary technology:** Our 3D laser scanning and gait analysis tools are used to precisely map each customer's foot pressure, arch profile, and gait mechanics. These tools cannot be replicated at home or at a generic shoe retailer.

**In-house manufacturing:** Custom orthotics are manufactured in-store using environmentally-friendly 3D printing. Turnaround is faster than a traditional podiatric orthotic lab.
