# Foot Solutions — Products

## Custom Orthotics

**Price:** $400–$500 (varies by complexity and design)
**Availability:** In-store only. Requires a foot & gait analysis consultation.
**Technology:** 3D laser scanning + digital modeling + in-house 3D printing
**Lifespan:** 3+ years with proper care
**HSA/FSA:** Often covered — check your plan
**Adjustment policy:** In-house modifications included if fit needs adjustment

Custom orthotics are Foot Solutions' flagship product. They are individually designed by a board-certified pedorthist after a full evaluation — not selected from a prefab library. Every element is chosen for the individual customer: arch height, heel cup depth, metatarsal pad placement, top cover material, and cushioning density.

A custom orthotic addresses the root biomechanical cause of pain. Off-the-shelf insoles treat symptoms.
